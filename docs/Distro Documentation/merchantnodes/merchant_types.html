You may have noticed that there are three healer and two banker NPCs. The wanderinghealerwill only resurrect a dead player who is not a criminal or murderer.  The normal healer NPC will not heal criminals and murderers but the BucsHealer will heal anyone regardless of their criminal or murderer status. The same principle applies to the normal banker and bucsbanker. The regular banker will not do business with criminals and murderers but the bucsbanker will do business with anyone.
